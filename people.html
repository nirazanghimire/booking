➔ Adarsha Ojha | Senior Travel Consultant

Adarsha has been working in the travel industry since 1998 and has a broad knowledge of various destinations. He describes his most memorable travel experience to date as being fortunate enough to have attended the unveiling of Virgin Galactic’s Whitenight II & Spaceship II design’s in NYC. He was lucky enough to meet Sir Richard Branson, explore his favourite city of New York and mingle with many high profile personalities. During the launch Adarsha had the chance to ride in a Centrifuge, which is a simulated spaceship. The experience was exhilarating and one he will never forget. Other travel highlights for Adarsha include experiencing New Year’s Eve in Times Square, elephant trekking in India and soaking up the sun in Jamaica!! Adarsha’s first love is his family, wife Rojina, daughter Adrisha and son, Rodarsha. Her family would be on the top of his list when choosing his dream destination and he hopes to one day travel with them to the Greek Islands and enjoy the beauty in this part of the world.
